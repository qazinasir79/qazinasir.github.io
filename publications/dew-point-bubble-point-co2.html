Measurement and Phase Behavior Modeling (Dew Point + Bubble Point) of CO₂ Rich Gas Mixture

Qazi Nasir, Khalik M. Sabil, Khashayar Nasrifar

Paper

Abstract

This paper presents experimental measurements and thermodynamic modeling of dew point and bubble point phase behavior for CO₂-rich gas mixtures. Accurate phase behavior data are essential for designing separation processes and pipeline transportation systems for CO₂-rich natural gas.

Phase equilibrium data were measured using a visual PVT cell over a range of temperatures and compositions. The experimental data were modeled using the Peng-Robinson equation of state with various mixing rules. The results demonstrate good agreement between experimental data and model predictions, providing reliable phase envelopes for process design.
